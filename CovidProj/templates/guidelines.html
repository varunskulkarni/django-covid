When to seek further medical advice.


  • If the following symptoms and signs develop.
      1. Difficulty in breathing.
      2. Oxygen saturation ≤ 94% using fingertip pulse oximeter.
      3. Persistent fever of ≥380 C (100.40 F) for more than 24 hours.
      4. Persistent pain/pressure in the chest.
      5. Mental confusion or inability to arouse.
      6. Slurred speech/seizures.
      7. Weakness or numbness in any limb or face.
      8. Developing bluish discolorations of lips/face.
      9. Any other symptom the person considers serious.
      10. As advised by treating physician.

Instructions to the person in home isolation.


  •   1. The person shall wear medical facemask/ N-95 facemask at all times.
      2. The person must stay in the identified room only and maintain a physical distance of 2     meters/ 6 feet.
      3. The person shall take rest and drink a lot of fluids to maintain adequate hydration.
      4. Shall follow cough etiquettes at all times.
      5. Hands shall be washed often with soap and water for at least 40 seconds
      6. Do not share personal items like utensils, towels, etc. with other family members. Keep them     separate.
      7. Clean surfaces in the room that are touched often.
      8. Clean and disinfect bathroom and toilet surfaces at least once daily.
      9. The person shall strictly follow the physician’s instructions and medication advice.
      10. The person shall self-monitor his/her health with a fingertip pulse oximeter and digital     thermometer daily.
      11. The person shall report promptly if he/she develops any worsening of symptoms.
      12. The person shall take healthy and nutritious diet.
      13. Smoking, chewing tobacco and alcohol intake shall be strictly avoided.
      14. The person shall receive counselling services when necessary.
      15.Download Arogya Setu App on mobile (available at Aarogya-Setu-App) and it should     remain active at all times   (through Bluetooth and Wi-Fi).

Instructions to the family members of person in home isolation.


  •   1. Do not panic. Do not stigmatise.
      2. Keep the person cheerful and boost their morale.
      3. Ensure that the person is in strict home isolation.
      4. Maintain a physical distance of at least 2 metres/6 feet.
      5. Visitors are strictly not allowed until the person has completely recovered and has no signs or     symptoms of COVID-19.
      6. Remember, the fight is against the disease and not the person.
      7. For any assistance, please call Apthamitra helpline – 14410.

Instructions to neighbours of persons who are home isolated.


  •   1. Do not panic. Do not stigmatise.
      2. Support the person and his/her family by providing essential items like medicines, rations,     vegetables, etc. until they get cured as may be required.
      3. Keep a vigil on the person in home isolation to ensure strict home isolation.
      4. Maintain a physical distance of at least 2 metres/6 feet.
      5. Remember, the fight is against the disease and not the person.
      6. For any assistance, please call Apthamitra helpline – 14410.

Model diet plan.


  •   1. Eat whole grains such as brown rice, whole wheat flour, oats, millets, etc.
      2. Include beans, lentils & pulses as these are good sources of protein.
      3. Include fresh fruits & vegetables (Bright coloured fruits & vegetables like red capsicum, carrots,     beetroot & greens etc).
      4. Drink 8-10 Glasses of water and Hydrate yourself. Water helps to flush out toxins.
      5. Citrus fruits like lemons & oranges are a good source of Vit C which is key in improving     immunity levels & to fight off infections.
      6. Include spices like ginger, garlic & turmeric which are natural immunity boosters.
      7. Eat home-cooked food. Use low fat and less oil for cooking food.
      8. Wash fruits & vegetables before use Include Low-fat milk & yogurt as they are good sources of     protein & calcium.
      8. Strictly avoid alcoholic drinks.
